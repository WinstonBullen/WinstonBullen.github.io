Ball Detection

Computer Vision for High School Robotics

I was a member of the computer vision subteam on my high school's robotics team. My primary contribution was a full ball detection algorithm. The program took color and depth feeds from an Intel Realsense D435 and returned the distance and yaw to the closest ball in frame. This was crucial for efficient gathering of the balls that scored points in competition. My smaller contributions include design, engineering, and bug fixes for other software.

Being a part of the robotics team was very fulfilling. I loved the collaboration, learning, and technical challenge of competition. Accomplishing my goals in a timely manner was rewarding. I missed robotics so much that I recently joined the UW Huskies Robotics team. I look forward to continuing my work in computer vision.

Git Repository: https://github.com/WinstonBullen/High-School-Vision-Robotics

Simulated Autonomous Vehicles

Deep & Reinforcement Learning for Simulated Self-Driving

Motion Detection

Capturing Images of Movement via Webcam

My favorite day project is a motion detector that I wrote to show my grandfather how to code. The program is simple: capture images from a webcam when motion is detected through the webcam video. The central algorithm involves breaking each frame into image segments to detect clusters of movement that could be an animal or human. This makes small disruptions unlikely to trigger the image capture. I made it from scratch in a few hours.

Git Repository: https://github.com/WinstonBullen/Motion-Detector

  • C++
  • OpenCV

I.D. Detection

Capturing Images of I.D. Cards via Webcam

My second favorite day project from high school is an I.D. card detector. The program captures an image of the I.D. card seen through the webcam video feed. The central algorithm involves thresholding by specific card features such as color and dimensions. The program is only guaranteed to work with my high school's I.D. format, but only minor modifications are necessary for another format.

Git Repository: https://github.com/WinstonBullen/ID-Card-Detector
