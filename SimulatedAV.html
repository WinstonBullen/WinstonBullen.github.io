Simulated Autonomous Vehicles

Deep & Reinforcement Learning for Simulated Self-Driving

I started studying artificial intelligence in 2019 by taking some online courses and reading two reference books. One of those books had several chapters dedicated to training models for autonomous vehcicles in a simulated environment. I quickly became engrossed in the challenge of autonomous driving.

My first project was training a convolutional neural network for the AirSim simulation in the Unreal Engine. This was followed by another project using deep reinforcement learning for the same environment. My latest project was training a reinforcement learning model for the AWS DeepRacer simulation. These small but practical projects are just the start to my exploration of autonomous driving.

Git Repository: https://github.com/WinstonBullen/Simulated-Autonomous-Vehicles
