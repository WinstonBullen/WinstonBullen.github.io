About Me

Winston Bullen

I was born at the University of Washington Medical Center in Seattle. I started coding in my sophomore year of high school when I bought a book on web development. I quickly fell in love with computer science, doing some interesting projects to explore the field. My favorite projects are displayed on this website accompanied by their Git repositories.

I returned to my birthplace, UW, for my undergraduate in September 2020. I am currently pursuing my B.S. in Computer Science with an expected graduation date of June 2024. I am most interested in artificial intelligence, computer vision, and robotics. Please feel free to contact me via email to discuss any of these topics.
